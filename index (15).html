Lauren Ballard

Filmmaking

Lauren is a filmmaker with a passion for telling powerful, personal stories that center underrepresented voices. With a background in directing and screenwriting, she brings a sharp eye for emotion and a deep respect for authenticity to every project. Whether she’s crafting coming-of-age dramas or experimental shorts, Lauren’s goal is always the same: to make people feel, think, and connect.

A junior majoring in filmmaking, Lauren has written and directed a range of short films—from experimental dreamscapes to grounded dramas—each shaped by her distinct visual style and heartfelt storytelling. She draws inspiration from early 2000s Black cinema, blending nostalgic tones with modern themes and warm, soulful aesthetics. Whether she’s behind the camera or in the editing room, Lauren brings intention to every frame and authenticity to every character.

Currently interning with Digital4Good, a nonprofit focused on youth storytelling and social impact, Lauren continues to grow as a filmmaker committed to using her voice for good. Her long-term vision is to direct powerful films that reflect real life, amplify marginalized experiences, and help others feel seen.
